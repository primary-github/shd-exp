**Emergent properties:** An organism is composed of various parts, from molecules and atoms to organs and organ systems. However, the collective behaviour and properties of the organism are not just the sum of these individual components. For instance, while a human is made up of these parts, it exhibits complex behaviours, such as self-reflection, that its individual components may not possess.
